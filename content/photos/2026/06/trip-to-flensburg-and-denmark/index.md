I initially wanted to cancel this trip, but eventually decided against it. It was the right decision. Apart from some bad luck with the weather, it was s very nice little trip – and direly needed!

I made Flensburg my "base of operations" and went on day trips around the area. Apart from Flensburg, I visited Glücksburg and Sønderborg.

More to come, I guess. Still cannot shake the „Fernweh“.

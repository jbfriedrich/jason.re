I thought about getting “Ghost Recon: Breakpoint”, but after reading this Reddit and Kotaku posts, and watching the video below, I am seriously reconsidering. I guess I will wait it out and buy some kind of “Ultimate Edition” on sale in a year or so. The game will have matured, be patched and most of the content will be released by then.

I will wait and see what other reviewers write about the game, especially ACG and the Games Podcast. And then make a final decision.
